Welcome to Doob Privacy Policy

At Nemat Consultancy, we are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of our clients, candidates, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. By engaging with our services or accessing our website, you consent to the practices described below.


Information Collection:

We collect personal information, such as names, contact details, and professional backgrounds, to provide our HR consultancy services effectively. This information is obtained through voluntary submissions, third-party sources, and our website forms.


Use of Information:

We use the collected information to match candidates with suitable job opportunities, assist clients in their recruitment processes, and deliver personalized HR solutions. Your information may also be used for communication purposes, including updates, notifications, and marketing materials, with the option to opt out at any time.


Data Protection:

We implement strict security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law.


Sharing of Information:

We may share your personal information with trusted third parties, such as our clients and partners, solely for the purpose of facilitating recruitment processes and delivering our services. We do not sell, rent, or disclose your information to any other external parties without your consent, unless required by law.


Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. These technologies collect non-personal information such as IP addresses, browser types, and website usage patterns. You can adjust your browser settings to manage or disable cookies if desired.


External Links:

Our website may contain links to external websites. Please note that we are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies before providing any personal information.


Consent and Updates:

By using our services or accessing our website, you consent to the terms of this Privacy Policy. We may update this policy periodically, and any modifications will be effective upon posting on our website.



If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Privacy Policy or the use of your personal information, please contact us using the provided contact information.
